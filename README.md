## 🖌 Overview  

Todoist Professional Suite is a comprehensive productivity and project management platform that serves individuals and teams with a task organization environment that scales from simple personal to-do lists through complex multi-project team workflows.

Team project collaboration allows shared projects with task assignment, comment threads, and activity logs that keep every team member informed about project status without requiring separate project management software for small to medium team workflows.

Goals and habit tracking in Todoist Professional Suite extend task management into behavioral accountability, letting you define recurring objectives and track consistency over time alongside the project tasks that occupy your daily work.

Filter and label systems create custom views of your task database that surface the right tasks for any context — by project, priority, date, tag, or any combination — making it easy to focus on what matters most at any given moment.

Todoist Professional Suite is the task management platform for users who have tried many productivity systems and found that consistent, reliable capture combined with flexible organization and genuine cross-platform sync is what actually makes a task manager work in practice.
